PRIVACY POLICY

Effective date: March 24, 2026

everysong.site ("the Service") is a music metadata search tool. This policy describes what data we collect and how we use it.

What we collect

If you register for an API key, we collect your email address. We store it alongside your API key and a count of daily requests. We do not collect any other personal information. The site itself uses no cookies, no analytics, and no tracking scripts. Favorites and theme preferences are stored in your browser's localStorage and never sent to our servers.

How we use your data

Your email is used solely to associate your API key with an account. Daily request counts are used to enforce rate limits. We do not sell, share, or disclose your email to any third party.

Data retention

API key records are retained as long as the key is active. If you want your data deleted, email the address listed on the GitHub repository and we will remove your record.

Third-party services

The site is hosted on Vercel. API requests are proxied through Vercel to our database server. Vercel's privacy policy applies to their infrastructure. We do not use any other third-party services.

Music data

The database contains publicly available metadata (artist name, track title, BPM, key, popularity, speechiness, instrumentalness, time signature). No audio content is stored or served.

Changes

We may update this policy. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.
